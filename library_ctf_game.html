Library ctf game

Welcome to the library capture the flag (ctf) game. The goal of this game is to take the player (you) through the process of doing library research. We will take a subject and develop it into a topic, find basic information about it, and research it using books from the library catalog, the library journal databases, and the web.

Instructions:

We have broken down the library research process into five sections below. Click each of the sections to find a series of fill-in the blank questions and a flag field.

At the top of each section there is a walkthrough icon. Click the icon to open a new page. As you read the walkthrough, answer the questions by filling the blank and clicking the submit answer button.

At the end of the walkthrough there will be a capture-the-flag exercise. Follow the instructions correctly and you will find a 30 digit flag. Copy this number, paste it in the flag field, and submit to complete the section.

By the end of the five sections, you will find a final flag that you will copy and send to your instructor to let them know you have completed the game.
